Every engineer has been made for some particular work, and the desire for that work has been put in every heart. Humans have always thrived to invent new and better technology. Technology is the tomorrows answer for today's questions !!! For all those hearts that wish to pursue a desire in a field related to robotics right in our own college, it’s here “THE ROBOTICS CLUB @ SASTRA”.


Founded in the year 2008, under the guidance of Dr. Anjan Kumar Dash , The Club has successfully provided the required environment in stimulating the interest and encouraging students towards exploration, design, and development of their ideas irrespective of their field of study.

The Robotics Club has committed to provide an active platform for students to display and develop their practical skills and knowledge, also offering indispensable guidance, workshops and tutorials along with tools, equipment and work-space.

Thinking out of the box, exploring new project ideas, creativeness and innovation are the best team to create a whole new invasion in the field of robotics.

Join us and give your robotic thought a life.
